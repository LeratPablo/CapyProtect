Because Life is Better with a Capy-buddy by Your Side !

We are thrilled to introduce CapyProtect, an association dedicated to the protection and conservation of our adorable capybara friends.
Here at CapyProtect, we believe in combining a serious mission with a lighthearted approach, because let's face it – capybaras make everything better!

The Capybara

Welcome to the wild and wonderful world of capybaras !
These furry, adorable creatures look like a cross between a watermelon and a teddy bear, with breathtaking swimming skills. Let's dive into their world and discover what makes them the ultimate experts in the art of chillaxing!

The Masters of Chillin' and the Kings of Cuteness !

Capybaras are the lords of relaxation. You'll often find them hanging out in marshes, swamps, and riverbanks, living life in the slow lane with a cocktail of grass and aquatic plants. They've got the prime real estate on lockdown, enjoying the sun while sipping on a refreshing Mocochinchi (okay, maybe not the cocktail part).

The Art of Hydrotherapy !

Capybaras have their own version of hydrotherapy, and it's called "being a capybara."
These born swimmers are the envy of Olympic athletes. Whether they're gliding gracefully through the water or treating themselves to a spa day with a group mud bath, they know how to make a splash and keep their fur sparkling.

The Coolest Love Story Ever !

When it comes to love, capybaras take it slow and steady.
They prove that love is not just for humans. You can see these capybara couples snuggling, sharing a tender moment or engaging in an exciting game of "Who can balance a flower on their head the longest ?
Love is truly in the air when capybaras are close.

Uniting All Species !

Capybaras have a knack for bringing different animals together.
You'll often spot birds perched on their backs, enjoying the view and having deep conversations about the meaning of life.
They're the diplomats of the animal kingdom, spreading peace, harmony, and the occasional leafy snack.

Uniting All Species !

If you want to learn a thing or two from capybaras, here are their top life lessons:

  • Embrace the art of relaxation and take naps like a pro.

  • Surround yourself with good company – laughter is always better with friends.

  • Practice the art of self-care, whether it's a spa day or a mud bath.

  • Spread love, kindness, and the occasional flower on someone's head.

So, join the capybara revolution, my friend!

Let's embrace their chill vibes, their cuddly nature, and their ability to bring joy to the world.

Remember, when life gets tough, just ask yourself, "What would a capybara do ?"

And then go take a nap.
